Background

Fidhul Krishna is an embedded systems engineer based in Kerala, India, specializing in IoT firmware development, BLE protocol design, and hardware-software co-design. He approaches technical problems at the intersection of circuit design and systems thinking — building devices that work reliably in real-world conditions, not just on a bench.

Ventures & Teaching

In 2024, Fidhul co-founded Zorift alongside Yadhusnav — a technology venture developing connected products within the KSUM startup ecosystem. Beyond building systems, he actively teaches IoT and embedded programming to the next generation of engineers, maintaining a technical presence on Fidhul Official and across GitHub.
