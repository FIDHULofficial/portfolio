About Me

The Mind Behind the Machine

I'm Fidhul Krishna, a KPESH student with a passion for turning wild ideas into real-world devices. I live and breathe IoT — from wiring up ESP32 boards to coding Python scripts that make laptops respond to hand gestures like Iron Man.

I'm a device engineer who builds from scratch. Give me an Arduino, a microcontroller, or even just a mobile phone, and I'll turn it into something extraordinary. I believe in learning by doing — and then teaching others what I've learned.

When I'm not soldering circuits or deploying code, I'm exploring video editing, experimenting with music production on BandLab, or brainstorming the next big product at Zorift — the startup I co-founded.

I document my journey on YouTube at Fidhul Official, where I share how I build things from scrap and turn them into working tech. Every project is a story of curiosity, grit, and a bit of controlled chaos.
